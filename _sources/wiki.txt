==========
PyGMI Wiki
==========
The wiki will be where you can find information about how to use PyGMI. Please
make sure you check out the quick start, since some important operational
issues and fundamental principles are covered there.

Remember the following:

* Use the menus to insert an object.
* Use the line connector on the toolbar to connect objects.
* **Double-click** to run or activate an object.
* **Right-click** to query an object, displaying graphs, images and statistics.
