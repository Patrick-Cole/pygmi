Profile Picture Importer

The profile editor allows for the display of a picture along the profile. The importer allows for a simple set of coordinates to be assigned to the picture.

Options

  • Profile Direction - The profile can be defined as being west to east or south to north.
  • West/South Coordinate - This is the coordinate assigned to the left side of the image.
  • East/North Coordinate - This is the coordinate assigned to the right side of the image.
  • Maximum Altitude - this is the altitude at the top of the image.
  • Depth - This is the depth of the image.
