Tilt Depth Interpretation

The tilt depth interpretation is a techniques for calculating depths to vertical sided bodies from the magnetic tilt angle. As such it required magnetic data as input.

This works on the principle that the distance between the -45 degree and 45 degree tilt angle is twice the distance to the body. This zone of interest is highlighted on the map.

What is important to note is that this technique works best with data which has been reduced to the pole. For this reason, RTP has been built into the module.

Options

  • Band to perform tilt depth - this should be magnetic data
  • Color Bar - colors used to display the tilt angle data.
  • Inclination of Magnetic Field - inclination of the field at the location and date of survey.
  • Declination of Magnetic Field - declination of the field at the location and date of survey.
  • Recalculate Tilt Depth - used to recalculate after changing inclination or declination.
